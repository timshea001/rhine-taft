The Role of Expectation in Thought Projection

Notes on priming language and how it tilted first impressions.

Brief framing lines were read to some readers prior to the page, while others had no prep at all. The aim was to see if a hint or prime could nudge a reader toward a line that fits the frame.

Logs note that prep lines can steer recall, yet the same lines also add noise. The memo here lists prep text, room notes, and small charts, with care to keep the stance neutral.
